Your Protocol — Step by Step
1
💊 Medication
Do this first
Take your migraine medication immediately — before pain peaks. Triptans and similar medications are most effective early. Don't wait to see if it gets worse.
2:00:00
Until earliest re-dose
Counts down from medication time. Do not take a second dose before this reaches zero.
2
🚿 Hot Shower
~10 min
Get in the shower now while you still can tolerate movement. Hot water on neck and shoulders can reduce vascular tension. Don't skip this if you're early in the attack — it's harder later.
10:00
10 min shower timer. Start it and get in.
3
☕ Caffeine + Water
Small amount
Small amount of caffeine (half a coffee, tea, a caffeinated painkiller) can help constrict blood vessels and boost medication effectiveness. Drink at least one full glass of water with it — dehydration makes migraines worse.
4
❄️ Cold Compress + Dark Room
Set up now
Get your cold compress from the freezer. Blackout curtains closed, lights off, phone face down. Set your phone to Do Not Disturb. No screens — your eyes need darkness. Get everything you need before you lie down.
Checklist before lying down
5
😴 Rest / Sleep
As long as needed
Phone down. Eyes closed. Cold compress on forehead or neck. Sleep is the most effective migraine treatment once you're in it. Don't check messages. Don't plan. The only job right now is to rest.
